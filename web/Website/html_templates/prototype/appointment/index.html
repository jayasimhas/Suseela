Request an Appointment

Main Line Health is dedicated to providing prompt and attentive service. Online appointment requests are handled Monday through Friday, 8:00 am to 6:00 pm. One of our physician referral specialists will contact you within 1 business day to confirm an appointment date and time. if you are having an emergency, call 911 immediately or go to the nearest emergency room.
